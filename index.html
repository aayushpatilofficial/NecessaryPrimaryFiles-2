Rules & Regulations

  • Eligibility: Participation is open to students enrolled in recognized schools. Students can participate individually or in teams of up to 3 members. Each team/member can register only one project.
  • Registration: All participants must complete the registration form with accurate details. Registration is confirmed only after receiving the acknowledgement by filling the register form in the website. Last date for registration will be strictly followed; late entries will not be entertained.
  • Project Guidelines: Projects must align with the theme: “Sustainable Futures”. Originality is mandatory. Projects can be in any branch of science or technology. The projects should be compulsorily working. Safety protocols must be followed.
  • Judging Criteria: Projects will be evaluated on originality and creativity, scientific knowledge and accuracy, presentation and clarity, relevance to the theme, practicality and applicability.
  • Conduct: Participants must behave respectfully. Misconduct including cheating or misrepresentation may lead to disqualification. Participants must adhere to timelines and schedules.
  • Awards & Recognition: Certificates will be provided to all registered participants. Prizes for winners in each category. Winning projects may be showcased on official Scientrix platforms and social media.
  • Miscellaneous: Organizers reserve the right to make changes in rules or judging criteria at any time. Judges’ decisions are final. Any disputes will be resolved by the Scientrix organizing committee.
